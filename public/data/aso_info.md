## ASO fundamentals

### "Apple indexes: 
Title, Subtitle, Keyword Field, (not the description) [ordered by most important]

### Google indexes: 
Title, Short Description, Long Description, Reviews [ordered by most important]

Ranking Signals (after keywords)
| Signal            | Apple     | Google    |
| ----------------- | --------- | --------- |
| Keyword relevance | Very high | High      |
| Install velocity  | Very high | Very high |
| Ratings & reviews | High      | Very high |
| Retention         | Medium    | High      |
| CTR (tap-through) | High      | High      |
| Conversion rate   | High      | High      |

### Long-tail ASO opportunities: 
Autocomplete demand, Weak SERPs, Smart keyword placement" (chatgpt) or perhaps you use something like google keyword planner or other tools to identify keywords and then backtest them against the app store

"Opportunity Score (0–5):

+1 keyword appears in title of ≤2 apps
+1 avg reviews < 2,000
+1 avg rating < 4.3
+1 keyword length ≥ 3 words
+1 keyword appears in short description of ≤4 apps"
